About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Security Analyst to join our dynamic team. In this role, you'll be at the forefront of our security operations, working with cutting-edge technologies to protect our clients and infrastructure.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and analyze security events from various sources
  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing
  • Implement and maintain security controls and protocols
  • Perform OSINT investigations for threat intelligence
  • Develop and maintain security documentation
  • Respond to and investigate security incidents

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity roles
  • One or more certifications: CEH, Security+, CySA+, or equivalent
  • Proficiency in Python, Bash, and security tools
  • Strong understanding of cloud security (AWS/Azure/GCP)
  • Experience with SIEM solutions and security monitoring

Preferred Skills

  • Digital forensics and incident response experience
  • Advanced OSINT and threat intelligence gathering
  • Knowledge of security frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001)
  • Experience with container security
  • Scripting and automation skills
