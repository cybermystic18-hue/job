About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Security Analyst to join our dynamic Security Operations Center. In this role, you'll be at the forefront of our security operations, working with cutting-edge technologies to protect our clients and infrastructure from sophisticated cyber threats.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor and analyze security events from various sources including SIEM, EDR, and network sensors
  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and coordinate penetration testing activities
  • Implement and maintain security controls and protocols across cloud and on-prem environments
  • Perform OSINT investigations for threat intelligence and proactive defense
  • Develop and maintain comprehensive security documentation and procedures
  • Respond to and investigate security incidents following established playbooks
  • Mentor junior analysts and contribute to team knowledge sharing

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Cybersecurity, or related field
  • 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity roles with focus on security operations
  • One or more industry certifications: CEH, Security+, CySA+, GCIH, or equivalent
  • Proficiency in Python, Bash, and common security tools
  • Strong understanding of cloud security (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Experience with SIEM solutions and security monitoring platforms
  • Knowledge of network protocols, firewall configurations, and IDS/IPS systems

Preferred Skills

  • Digital forensics and incident response experience
  • Advanced OSINT and threat intelligence gathering techniques
  • Knowledge of security frameworks (NIST, ISO 27001, CIS Controls)
  • Experience with container security (Docker, Kubernetes)
  • Scripting and automation skills for security orchestration
  • Familiarity with threat hunting methodologies
